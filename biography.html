2010 - Mixtape "House of Balloons"

The Weeknd released his debut mixtape "House of Balloons," which received critical acclaim and drew attention for its dark, moody sound.

2015 - "Beauty Behind the Madness"

This album featured hits like "Can't Feel My Face" and "The Hills," propelling The Weeknd to international stardom.

2018 - "My Dear Melancholy"

With this project, The Weeknd returned to his darker roots, exploring themes of heartbreak and introspection.

2020 - "After Hours" and "Blinding Lights"

His album "After Hours" became a cultural phenomenon, with "Blinding Lights" becoming one of the biggest hits of the decade.

2016 - "Starboy"

The album "Starboy," featuring Daft Punk, was a commercial success and showcased The Weeknd's evolution in sound and style.

2022 - "Dawn FM"

In 2022, The Weeknd released "Dawn FM," a concept album with themes of purgatory, featuring collaborations with artists like Tyler, the Creator and Lil Wayne.
